3D Audio Visualizer Instructions

Welcome to the 3D Audio Visualizer. Follow these instructions to use the visualizer:

  1. Upload an audio/son by clicking the "Upload Audio" button and selecting a song file from your device.
  2. Once the song is uploaded, you can control the playback using the controls in the bottom pane:
    • Use the play/pause button to start or pause the song.
    • Use the speed control slider to adjust the playback speed of the song.
    • Use the volume control slider to adjust the sound level of the song.
