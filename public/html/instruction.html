3D Audio Visualizer Instructions

Welcome to the 3D Audio Visualizer. Follow these instructions to use the visualizer:

  1. Upload an audio/son by clicking the "Upload Audio" button and selecting a song file from your device.
  2. There is a black tweakpane dashboard in the top right corner
    • Use the volume control slider to adjust the sound level of the song.
    • Click on the colored square box for background to change the body color.
    • Click on the colored square box for ball to change the ball mesh color.
    • Use the speed control slider to adjust the playback speed of the song.
  3. Controls in the bottom pane:
    • Use the play/pause button to start or pause the audio.
    • Use the playback control slider to return back to a part of the audio.
    • Use the volume control slider to adjust the sound level of the audio.
