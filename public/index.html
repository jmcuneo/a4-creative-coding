3D Audio Visualizer Controls

Welcome to the Audio Visualizer made by Zesh! Here's how to control the audio visualization:

  • Upload Audio: Click on 'Choose File' to load your audio file.
  • Particle Size: Use the slider to adjust the size of the particles.
  • Torus Knot Scale: Use the slider to scale the Torus Knot geometry.
  • Rotation Speed: Use the slider to adjust how fast the Torus Knot rotates.
  • Color Picker: Click on the color button to change the color of the Torus Knot.

Have fun!
